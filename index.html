Arknights is a mobile game developed by Hypergryph.
The game is set in the dystopian, post-apocalyptic future setting of the planet Terra,
where people exhibit characteristics of animals or mythological races.
Natural disasters leave behind a valuable mineral, originium, which infects people with a progressive disease,
Oripathy is the name, but also allows them to also use "Arts" (magic).
Because oripathy is highly infectious and invariably fatal, the infected are shunned and persecuted.
In response, some of them formed what they call :
The Reunion movement,
A militant group waging war on the despotic governments of Terra.

Abyssal Hunters


The Abyssal Hunters,
sometimes simply known as the Abyssal,
is an organization in Arknights whose mission is
to keep the sea monsters in check from their invasion of land.
Their eerie background makes them both mysterious and dangerous to the land-dwellers of Terra.
